Japan

Yokohama and Tokyo

Dive into fully unfamiliar culture. Eat new dishes, stare long at shelves in supermarkets, want to try everything. Walk and feel as a part of anime or manga series. Respect the calmness and patience. Inspect temples and old traditional buildings. Get tires of lights in Shindsuku and Ikebukuro. Get lost in the subway.

South Korea

Seoul

Feel the middle between traditions and modern ideas, how they mix, while respecting each other. To try traditional food: topokki,jajangmyeon, hoeddeok and rice puddings. Visit Joseong palaces and old parks. Watch traditional houses and may be get into one.

Italy

Milan

Enjoy life like Italians do. Try world famous cuisine. Pasta, pizza, gelatto and all, all others. Visit museums and basilicas. Feel trembling of artists in the ancient buildings. Want to be good in history, to fully apreciate the decor elements of buildings and small details in statues, mosaics and paintings.

United States

New York

To feel the american rush. Get lost in the busy streets and to watch stylish people going to do their important business. Eat unfamiliar food and sit in huge public libraries. Walk in Brooklin, go to china town, visit Metropolitan museum. Take a road trip through all states.

Austria

Vienna

Take plane to this city to have a walk through Christmas markets. When leaves of trees are still yellow, but everyone are getting ready for holidays season. Scent of baking cookies, spiced drinks and warm clothes. Long walks, stopping near vintage shops and book stores. Mix of European buildings, which seems to be artificial and old stories.

France

Paris

The city which everybody should visit at least once in lifetime. Streets, squares, gardens and cafes glorified by writers, poets, photographers and by all kinds of artists. Place where millions of people have found their inspiration. City where all actions can be made as a part of the performance. Parisian style, way of life, which many people follow and try to copy, in its hometown.
